About Success & Unity School Jos

Our History Our Mission Our Leadership

Our Story & Philosophy

Success & Unity School Jos was founded in 2005 with a vision to create an educational institution that would serve as a beacon of excellence in Plateau State and beyond.

Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that every child has unique potential waiting to be discovered. We combine academic rigor with character development to prepare students for success in all aspects of life.

Over the decades, we have grown from a small primary school to a comprehensive educational institution offering both primary and secondary education. Our alumni network spans across Nigeria and the world, with graduates making significant contributions in various fields.

We believe in educating the whole child - intellectually, emotionally, socially, and morally. Our holistic approach ensures that students leave our institution not just with academic qualifications, but with the values and skills needed to navigate an increasingly complex world.

Our Journey Through Time

Two decades of educational excellence and continuous growth

2005

Foundation Established

Success & Unity Schools Jos was founded by Mr. Samaila Iliya Agwom. Started with just 20 students and 5 teachers in a modest building along Lamingo, Juth Road.

2008

Secondary School Addition

Expanded to include secondary education, becoming a full-fledged K-12 institution. The first batch of WAEC candidates recorded excellent results, setting a standard for academic excellence.

2010

New Campus Inauguration

Moved to our current 2-acre campus in Kyan-Rikkos with modern facilities including science laboratories, computer lab, and a well-equipped library.

2015

International Recognition

Awarded "Best School in Northern Nigeria" by the Federal Ministry of Education. Established partnerships with international educational organizations.

2020

Digital Transformation

Implemented comprehensive digital learning infrastructure. Launched virtual classrooms and online learning platforms to enhance educational delivery.

2025

20th Anniversary & Future Vision

Celebrating 20 years of educational excellence. Launching new STEM programs and innovation lab to prepare students for 21st century challenges.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Success & Unity Schools

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in teaching, learning, and all school activities. Our commitment to excellence drives continuous improvement and outstanding results.

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards, promoting honesty, fairness, and responsibility in all our actions and relationships within the school community.

Respect

We value diversity and treat all individuals with dignity and respect, fostering an inclusive environment where every voice is heard and valued.

Innovation

We embrace creativity and innovation in teaching methods, curriculum development, and problem-solving to prepare students for a rapidly changing world.

Community

We believe in the power of community and actively engage parents, alumni, and the wider community in our educational mission and activities.

Sustainability

We are committed to environmental stewardship and sustainable practices, educating students to be responsible global citizens.
