Directions to Campus

  • From Jos Main City: Take Bauchi Road towards Kyan-Rikkos
  • From Airport Road: Head towards Rantya, then take the turn to Kyan-Rikkos

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting our school

Official visiting hours are Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. All visitors must register at the security gate and obtain a visitor's pass. For meetings with specific staff members, we recommend scheduling an appointment in advance.

Admission applications can be submitted online through our website or in person at the admissions office. You'll need to provide previous school records, birth certificate, passport photographs, and complete an entrance examination. For specific requirements for different classes, please contact the admissions office directly at admissions@sussjos.com or call +234 807 329 5314.

We strive to respond to all emails within 24-48 hours during working days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling the relevant department directly. Emails received on weekends or public holidays will be addressed on the next working day.

School fees can be paid through bank transfer, online payment portal, or directly at the accounts office. For bank transfers, use account details: Success & Unity Schools Jos, First Bank PLC, Account Number: 2034567890. Receipts should be presented at the accounts office for confirmation. Installment payment plans are available upon request.
